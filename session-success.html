Before We Begin

Informed consent

Please take a moment to read through the following before your session. This ensures you understand the nature of RTT™ hypnotherapy and feel fully informed and comfortable moving forward.

Please Read & Acknowledge

Nature of the work. RTT™ (Rapid Transformational Therapy) combines hypnotherapy, neuroscience, and psychotherapeutic techniques to help identify and address the subconscious roots of patterns, beliefs, and behaviours. Sessions involve a guided relaxation/hypnosis state in which we explore relevant memories and beliefs, followed by a personalised recording for you to listen to afterward.

Not a substitute for medical or psychiatric care. RTT™ hypnotherapy is a complementary modality intended to support personal growth and is not a substitute for medical, psychiatric, or psychological treatment. It is not a diagnosis, treatment, or cure for any medical or mental health condition. If you are currently under the care of a doctor, therapist, or psychiatrist, please consult them before beginning this work, and continue any prescribed treatment.

Hypnosis is a natural, safe state. Hypnosis is simply a deeply relaxed, focused state of attention — similar to daydreaming or the moments just before sleep. You remain in control throughout and can hear, speak, and end the session at any time.

Results vary. Every individual responds differently, and results are not guaranteed. The depth of transformation depends on many factors including your openness, willingness to engage with the process, and consistency in listening to your personalised recording afterward.

Confidentiality. Everything shared during your intake and session is held in strict confidence. Information will not be shared with any third party except where required by law, or where there is a risk of harm to yourself or others.

Recording. Your session itself is not recorded. A personalised 15-minute hypnosis audio is recorded separately for you, based on your session, for your continued use afterward. This audio is for your personal use only and will be stored securely.

Voluntary participation. Your participation in this session is entirely voluntary. You may pause, stop, or withdraw consent at any point before or during the session.

Emotional responses. Exploring memories, beliefs, and subconscious patterns can surface a range of emotional responses, including feelings that may be uncomfortable or unexpected. This is a normal part of the process and does not indicate that anything has gone wrong. If you have a history of trauma, significant mental health concerns, or are uncertain whether this work is appropriate for you, please consult a licensed medical or mental health professional before proceeding.

Limitation of liability. By proceeding, you acknowledge that you are participating in this session entirely of your own free will and at your own discretion. Isis Anchalee, in her capacity as an RTT™ practitioner, shall not be held liable for any emotional, psychological, physical, or other outcome — including any feelings of distress or trauma — that may arise during or after this session or from use of your personalised audio. You assume full responsibility for your own wellbeing and for seeking appropriate professional support where needed.
