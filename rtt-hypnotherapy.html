FAQ

Your questions

Will I be in control during hypnosis?
Absolutely. Hypnosis is simply a deeply relaxed, focused state — like the feeling just before sleep. You are fully aware, fully conscious, and in complete control throughout. You cannot be made to do or say anything against your will.
How quickly will I notice results?
Everyone experiences RTT™ differently. Some people feel shifts and a release of energetic blockages immediately during or after the session. Others begin to notice conscious changes after a few weeks of listening to their recording daily. And many people experience results they don't consciously register at first — their behaviour simply begins to change naturally, and they realise later how much has shifted. The transformation is working whether you can feel it or not.
Is this done online or in person?
Sessions are conducted online via Zoom. All you need is a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed.
How many sessions will I need?
Most issues resolve in 1–3 sessions. Simple, single-issue work often shifts in one session. More layered or complex patterns may benefit from 2–3. I will give you an honest recommendation after your first session.
What should I do before my session?
Come rested and hydrated, with a clear intention of what you want to change. Make sure your devices are fully charged or plugged in, notifications are on do not disturb, and you are in a comfortable, private space with a strong wifi connection where you will not be interrupted. The depth of your openness and willingness is the greatest predictor of your transformation.
Do I have to book a full session to get started?
Not at all. If you'd like to experience the work before committing to a 1:1 session, explore the pre-recorded hypnosis audio library — available instantly, no booking required.
